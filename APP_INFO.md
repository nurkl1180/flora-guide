FloraGuide is your AI-powered gardening assistant. This application leverages the advanced capabilities of Gemini 3.1 Pro to help you identify plants and provide expert care instructions.

Key Features:

Instant Plant Identification: Snap a photo or upload an image to identify any plant. The AI provides the common name, scientific name, and a detailed description.
Comprehensive Care Guides: Get tailored advice on planting, watering schedules, and nutrient requirements.
Edible Plant Support: For edible plants, the app provides specific growth milestones and harvesting tips.
Pest & Disease Management: Learn about common issues for your specific plant, along with prevention and treatment strategies.
AI Gardening Chatbot: A dedicated chat interface where you can ask any gardening questions, from soil pH to seasonal pruning.
Organic Design: A warm, approachable interface designed with a "Garden Organic" aesthetic, making it easy to use while you're out in the dirt.

How to use:
Identify: Click "Take Photo" or "Upload" on the home screen.
Learn: Once identified, scroll through the detailed care sections.
Chat: Click the message icon in the bottom right to ask the AI gardener specific questions about your plants.

The app is fully responsive and ready for use on both desktop and mobile devices.
